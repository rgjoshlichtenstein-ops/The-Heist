The Meters
Plan
How intact the original brief is. As it drops, commands that require a functioning plan lock out. Below 30, the operation is largely improvised.
Heat
How much attention the operation has attracted. As it rises, social commands lock out. Above 70 you are already a person of interest. Hard to bring back down.
Control
Crew operational composure. As it drops, commands requiring precision and steadiness lock out.
Window
Time remaining before the critical threshold. Every scene costs window. Below 35 it pulses. Cannot be recovered.
Locked Cards
When a card shows a word in the corner and goes gray, it cannot be played as your command. It can still be used as a support card.
